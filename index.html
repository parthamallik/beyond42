The origin Story

The debut Beyond42.195 was launched in 2020 to celebrate the 50th birthday of one of our running friends, who shared with us his dream of running his first 50km ultra marathon on his milestone day. Soon several of us marathoners got together to make his dream a reality. All runners seek a single non-repeating loop, traffic-free route. After a fervent search, we struck gold and found exactly what we wanted all within Bangalore...a run within the city with a strong flavour of countryside! The Beyond42.195 is indeed a scenic route, dotted with a rustic village life, with farmers tilling their land, with melodious chirping of birds and sounds of animals, lined with trees, and a soothing carpet of green as far as the eye can see. A year later the nostalgia lingers & we now aim to share this experience with the Bangalore running community this year with the 2nd edition of Beyond42.195

FAQs

Is there a prize money ?

A unique experience will be your reward. Of course, bragging rights go along with the turf!

What hydration support will be provided ?

We aim to make your experience custom. Along with providing the usual energy drinks, fruits and other fare, we’ll be happy to hold any specific items you’d like to drop off with us for consumption at any aid station. Yes, if you drop off biryani to eat at kilometer 30, we’ll hand it to you at the nearest aid station

Is my registration money refundable ?

Fortunately for you, we are prepping for the event weeks/months in advance. So, just like you, your registration money is already hard at work prepping for the event. As such, refunds won’t be feasible.

Will there be a drop-bag facility ?

Definitely, in your car at the start/end point. More importantly, we offer a unique ‘drop-off’ facility for your hydration/nutrition needs at our aid stations.
